REGISTRATION POLICY .

1. Registration.

Each participant must use a separate registration form. Registration is not transferable to another person. Registration confirmations are auto-generated and sent to the email address provided during the registration process once the payment transactions are completed successfully. For this purpose, it is COMPULSORY to fill in the email address clearly. If you did not receive your confirmation, please email: madphsmy@gmail.com (email Subject: Registration)

All poster presenters must register for the main conference upon acceptance of abstract. Last date for registration for poster presenters will be on 10th August 2019.


2. Payment.

Registration will only be valid when payment is received in full by the Malaysian Association of Dental Public Health Specialists (MADPHS).
   ~ Early bird registration fees will only be accepted until 11.59 pm 15 June 2019.
Payment made on or after 16 June 2019 will be charged as late/ onsite registration fees.
   ~ Online payment will only be accepted until 11.59 pm 15 August 2019.
   ~ Only CASH payment will be accepted for on-site registration of local and international delegates.
   ~ All payment is made at a fixed conversion rate of USD 1 = MYR 4.2.
